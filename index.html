KIWAN MAENG

Ph.D. Candidate · Electrical and Computer Engineering · Carnegie Mellon University
[CV]

I am a Ph.D. candidate studying computer engineering at CMU. My advisor is Professor Brandon Lucia. I am generally interested in co-designing compilers, new programming models, and runtime systems for emerging computing platforms. My current research focuses on energy-harvesting devices, which have no reliable power source (e.g., battery) attached and instead operates on a scarce and unpredictable energy gathered by its harvester (e.g., solar panel). I seek to broaden the possibilities of such devices.

I am also an amateur webtoonist. Check out my work if you know Korean. Otherwise, do not bother.
